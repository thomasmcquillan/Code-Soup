maker*

Designed around the Make/Use Template System - Maker* is a bespoke toolkit that helps you decide what to make and helps you gather the resources you need to begin.



Using  maker*  is easy.


Simply click the sleeve, neckline and body tiles of the garment model to cycle through styles. Your selections are used to determine how much fabric you will need to sew your chosen garment. If desired, you can enter custom measurements to find out your fabric needs - accommodating all body shapes and sizes.

Any templates needed for your design will be displayed. To download the templates, simply click on the links provided. If you wish, you can enter your email to have the instructions mailed to your inbox.
